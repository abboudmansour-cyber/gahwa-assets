Today's Signal
​
What happened
Washington downsized its Saudi footprint and Tehran struck a tanker in the same week — the old Gulf security playbook just got shredded.
**The US downsized its military presence in Saudi Arabia** after Riyadh refused airspace for Strait of Hormuz escorts, and Iran struck a Panama-flagged tanker — officials said — drawing a US retaliatory strike; both sides stepped back.
Next round of US-Iran talks is postponed until after Khamenei’s funeral on 9 July — any new Hormuz provocation could re-escalate overnight.
Watch
Iran threatens Hormuz shipping tolls from mid-August — if it materialises, oil markets will lurch again. · OPEC+ meets in July with Brent at $72; a production cut is firmly in play.

GCC & SAUDI
Neom Restructuring to Cost $16bn
**Webuild’s $4.7bn Trojena contract was terminated and fully reimbursed**, as Neom’s overhaul could saddle Saudi Arabia with up to $16bn in cancelled-contract costs. Contractors should re-calibrate their Saudi pipeline: Webuild is already bidding on new water and transport schemes now.
​
AI & TECH
MGX Raises $49bn for AI
**Abu Dhabi’s MGX closed a $49bn AI fund**, overshooting its $45bn target, and plans to deploy $10bn annually — already backing OpenAI, Anthropic, and xAI. MGX is also eyeing the acquisition of Singapore-based data centre operator DayOne; a deal that would reshape regional compute capacity right now.
​
GCC & SAUDI
IHC, Adani Ink $11.5bn Aluminium JV
**The UAE’s IHC and India’s Adani signed a $11.5bn joint venture** to build a greenfield aluminium complex in Odisha — the largest foreign direct investment in Indian metals. Construction will take over three years; firms that supply smelter tech or logistics should already be in the room.
​
CULTURE
WTA Finals Exit Riyadh
**Saudi Arabia’s WTA Finals stint ends after two seasons**, walking away from a $15.25mn prize pool even as the event set a record $5.3mn winner’s chequefor women’s sports. Tournament organisers are shopping for a new host this summer; Gulf neighbours may pounce on the branding opportunity now.
